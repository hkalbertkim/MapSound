# Commercial Licensing

MapSound uses a source-available Business Source License 1.1. Commercial usage requires a separate commercial license from the Licensor.

Commercial use includes, for example:

- hosted SaaS offerings
- paid APIs
- commercial redistribution
- embedding MapSound into a commercial platform
- internal enterprise production deployment
- white-label or reseller usage

For commercial licensing, contact [licensing@mapsound.me](mailto:licensing@mapsound.me).

Early commercial partners can discuss custom terms with the Licensor.
